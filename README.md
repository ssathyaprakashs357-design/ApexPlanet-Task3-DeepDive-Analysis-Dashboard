# Deep-Dive Analysis & Interactive Dashboarding

## Overview
This project was completed as part of my **Data Analyst Internship** at **ApexPlanet Software Pvt. Ltd.** The objective was to perform in-depth business analysis, define Key Performance Indicators (KPIs), and develop an interactive Power BI dashboard to support data-driven decision-making.

## Objectives
- Analyze business data to uncover meaningful insights.
- Define and evaluate key business KPIs.
- Build an interactive Power BI dashboard.
- Support strategic decision-making through data visualization.

## Key Deliverables
- Business KPI Analysis
- Deep-Dive Data Analysis
- Interactive Power BI Dashboard
- Business Insights & Recommendations
- Dashboard Presentation

## Tools & Technologies
- Power BI
- Microsoft Excel
- Data Visualization
- Business Intelligence
- KPI Analysis

## Dashboard Features
- KPI Cards
- Sales by Category
- Sales by City
- Product Performance Analysis
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Interactive Filters & Slicers

## Key Insights
- Electronics generated the highest sales.
- Kolkata recorded the highest city-wise sales.
- Sales peaked during March, June, October, and November.
- Customer distribution remained nearly balanced across genders.

## Business Recommendations
- Increase inventory for high-performing product categories.
- Strengthen promotional campaigns during peak sales months.
- Optimize marketing strategies for lower-performing categories.
- Expand successful sales strategies to additional regions.
- Continuously monitor KPIs to support business decisions.
